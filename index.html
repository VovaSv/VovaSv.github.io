Features

See below the main anabella's pump featers

Noiseless

Pump a milk without wake-up your baby.

Realistic

Immitating a baby's muscle system.

Adjustable

Adjustable speed and power.

Faster

Pump fast

The Anabella - a milk pumping device which gets almost all available breast milk, by imitating the muscle system of a baby's mouth. We managed to create a real tongue motion, along with the suction and other muscles of the mouth. This makes pumping breast milk much more effective and close to the real thing.
